Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our professional template development services.

What's included in the $200 starting price?

The starting price includes a basic custom template with up to 10 automation commands, basic error handling, and documentation. More complex templates with advanced features like AI integration, complex data processing, or multi-site automation may require additional investment.

How long does development typically take?

Most templates are completed within 5-7 business days. Complex projects requiring extensive custom logic or integration with multiple systems may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a timeline estimate after reviewing your requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! All templates include 30 days of support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages for continuous monitoring, updates, and enhancements as your needs evolve.

Can you work with websites that require login or have anti-bot measures?

Absolutely. Our experts specialize in handling complex scenarios including authentication, CAPTCHA solving, dynamic content, and anti-bot measures. We use advanced techniques and Selenix's AI capabilities to ensure reliable automation.

What if the template needs modifications after delivery?

Minor adjustments and bug fixes are included in the 30-day support period. For significant changes or additional features, we offer revision services at competitive rates. We'll always provide a clear quote before starting any additional work.

Do you offer training on how to use the custom templates?

Yes! Every template delivery includes comprehensive documentation and basic training. For teams or complex templates, we offer additional training sessions to ensure everyone can effectively use and maintain the automation.
