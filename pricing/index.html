Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Selenix and our support options.

Is Selenix really completely free?

Yes! Selenix is 100% free with no limitations on usage, features, or automations. The support plans are optional services for users who want professional assistance with setup, templates, or custom development.

What's the difference between support plans?

All plans include the same complete Selenix application. Professional Support adds priority email help and template installation. Custom Development includes creating specialized templates and integrations for your specific needs.

Do I need a support plan to use templates?

No! You can download and use community templates for free. Support plans help with installation and customization if you need hands-on assistance.

Can I cancel my support plan anytime?

Absolutely! Support plans are month-to-month with no contracts. You can cancel anytime and continue using Selenix for free.

What kind of custom templates can you create?

We can create templates for any website or workflow - from complex data scraping to multi-step form automation. Our team will work with you to build exactly what you need.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

Professional Support: 24-hour response time. Custom Development: 4-hour response time. Community users can get help through our forums and documentation.

Do support plans include training?

Professional Support includes basic guidance. Custom Development includes screen sharing sessions and comprehensive training on your custom solutions.

What if I'm not satisfied with my support plan?

Custom Development plans include a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. Professional Support can be cancelled anytime with no penalty.
