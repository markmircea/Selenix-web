Introduction to Selenix

Selenix is a powerful browser automation tool that allows you to automate repetitive tasks, scrape data, and interact with websites without writing a single line of code.

New to Selenix?

Check out our ready-made templates to quickly get started with common automation tasks.

What is Selenix?

Selenix is a visual browser automation tool that lets you record and automate workflows on any website. With its intuitive no-code interface, you can create powerful automations in minutes, even if you have no programming experience.

The Selenix interface showing a workflow builder

Key Features

No-Code Builder

Create automations visually with our drag-and-drop interface. No coding required.

Data Extraction

Easily extract data from websites and export it to CSV, Excel, or your favorite tools.

Scheduled Runs

Schedule your automations to run at specific times or trigger them based on events.

Templates

Get started quickly with our library of pre-built templates for common automation tasks.

Who is Selenix for?

Selenix is designed for anyone who wants to save time by automating repetitive tasks. Our users include:

  • Business Owners - Automate data entry, customer outreach, and market research
  • Marketers - Scrape competitor data, automate social media posts, and monitor campaigns
  • Data Analysts - Collect data from multiple sources without writing complex scripts
  • HR Professionals - Streamline candidate sourcing and application processes
  • E-commerce Managers - Monitor prices, track inventory, and update product listings
